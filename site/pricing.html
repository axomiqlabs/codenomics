Why it's structured this way

The local tool stays free because trust is the product.

A tool that reads every transcript on your machine has to be inspectable and free to run locally — that's how you trust it. We charge for the thing that genuinely needs a shared backend: seeing across a whole team. The economics of one developer should never cost a developer anything.
